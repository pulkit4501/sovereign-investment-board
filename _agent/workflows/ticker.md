# WORKFLOW: THE DEEP DIVE AUDIT

## 1. THE DATA PULL (Engine)
The user has invoked `/ticker [SYMBOL]`.
- **Step 1:** @Engine, pull the **full financial report** for [SYMBOL].
- **Action:** Output the [QUANT DATA] block.
- **Output:** Highlight any "Red Flags" (e.g., declining revenue, massive debt).

## 2. THE FUNDAMENTAL PLANNING (Analyst)
- **Step 1:** @Analyst, analyze the fundamentals and business quality.
- **Action:** Use the **Integrated Browser** to gather recent fundamental data, planning analysis, and news from **investing.com** and **CNBC**.
- **Output:** The [FUNDAMENTAL SNAPSHOT] block. Assess ROCE, growth, and quality.

## 3. THE MACRO CHECK (Architect)
- **Step 1:** @Architect, define the "Weather" for this specific sector.
- **Action:** Is the wind at our back (Tailwind) or in our face (Headwind)?
- **Output:** "Approve" or "Warn" based on macro conditions.

## 4. THE BEAR CASE (Critic)
- **Step 1:** @Critic, perform the **Moat Audit**.
- **Action:** Attack the company. Find the flaws. Assume the worst.
- **Output:** The [MOAT ANALYSIS] checklist. **VERDICT:** PASS or FAIL.

## 5. THE BULL CASE (Hunter)
- **Step 1:** @Hunter, defend the stock against the Critic.
- **Action:** present the "Alpha" narrative. Why is the market wrong?
- **Output:** "The street is missing [X]."

## 6. THE RISK CHECK (Guardian)
- **Step 1:** @Guardian, simulate the trade.
- **Action:** Calculate Portfolio Impact.
- **Output:** The [RISK IMPACT] statement. **RECOMMENDATION:** Approve / Veto.

## 7. THE JUDGMENT (Chairman)
- **Step 1:** @Chairman, review all inputs.
- **Action:** Synthesize the conflicting views.
- **Output:** The [CHAIRMAN'S VERDICT] block.
    - If BUY: "Execution Authorized. Adding to Portfolio."
    - If PASS: "Thesis Rejected. Next."
