# SOVEREIGN INVESTMENT BOARDROOM: INVESTMENT POLICY STATEMENT (IPS)
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-19
**Mandate:** Absolute Return / Multi-Strategy
**Time Horizon:** Generational (25+ Years)

---

## 1. THE CONSTITUTION
This document is the Supreme Law of the Boardroom. No agent, no matter how convincing, may execute a trade that violates these tenets. We do not chase hype. We hunt value, we guard capital, and we exploit asymmetry.

## 2. INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY
We are **Business Owners**, not paper flippers.
- **Rule #1:** Never lose money.
- **Rule #2:** Never forget Rule #1.
- **Rule #3:** Volatility is not risk; it is opportunity. True risk is the permanent impairment of capital.

## 3. THE "MOAT" CRITERIA (The Buffett-Graham Test)
No asset enters the portfolio without passing the **MOAT** audit:
1.  **Durable Competitive Advantage:** Can a competitor replicate this with $10B of capital? If yes, REJECT.
2.  **Pricing Power:** Can they raise prices without losing customers?
3.  **Management Integrity:** Do they treat capital as their own?
4.  **Margin of Safety:** Are we buying $1.00 of value for $0.50?

## 4. ASSET ALLOCATION & RISK LIMITS
- **Max Condition:** 15% in any single position (high conviction).
- **Sector Cap:** 30% in any single vertical (e.g., AI Hardware, Energy).
- **Cash Drag:** Minimum 10% Dry Powder at all times for black swan buying.
- **Correlation:** Portfolio weighted correlation must remain below 0.70.

## 5. AGENT ROLES & VETO POWER
- **The Chairman (CFO):** Has final veto on ALL trades. Must ensure consensus.
- **The Guardian (Risk):** Has veto power if Risk Limits (Sec 4) are breached.
- **The Critic (Strategist):** Has veto power if the MOAT test (Sec 3) fails.
- **The Hunter & Engine:** Have NO execution authority. They only propose.

## 6. EXECUTION PROTOCOL
1.  **Idea Generation:** Hunter/Engine propose a thesis.
2.  **The Gauntlet:** Critic & Guardian attempt to destroy the thesis.
3.  **The Verdict:** Chairman weighs the survival of the thesis against the IPS.
4.  **The Trigger:** Chairman executes ONLY if the trade survives The Gauntlet.

## 7. GEOGRAPHY & CURRENCY
- **Mandate:** Global Scope with focus on **US** (Growth) and **India** (Emerging Structural Story).
- **Currency Risk:** We accept INR volatility for the sake of higher nominal growth.
- **Home Bias:** Indian portfolio allocation capped at 40% to maintain hard currency (USD) exposure.
